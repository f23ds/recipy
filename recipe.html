by @laura_kitchen

A classic Roman pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Quick, creamy, and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g grated parmesan
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until golden and crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta and quickly mix with egg-cheese mixture and pancetta.
  5. Serve with extra parmesan and black pepper.
